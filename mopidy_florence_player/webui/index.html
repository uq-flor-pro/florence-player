Florence Player Station Management

On this page you can assign tracklists/internet radio stations to station switches, and upload audio to the player. For suggestions and inspiration in creating a playlist see Playlist for Life and BBC's Music Memories.

Here you can register a tracklist to play when a station button is pressed. Name each station with an alias and store the tracklist to play. If you want to play an artist you can find the artist's URI from their Spotify page.
